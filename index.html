Click Race

Welcome to Click Race! There are only a few simple rules to learning the game.

1. Your mouse is your controller. You will only be using your mouse to move around

2. When you start the game you will be given a goal, a timer, and a color to left click on

3. The color, is the color button you need to click to gain one point.

4. If you click the wrong color, you will lose 1 point from your score

5. Your goal will be the amount you need to win.

6. Good Luck!
